Journey Management Procedure
  1. Authorized Rest Areas
    • Designated safe parking locations with facilities
    • Must have 24-hour fuel/food, good lighting, and sufficient space
  2. RHM & JMP Development
    • Live document updated frequently with three phases: Planning, Executing, Closing
    • Includes risk assessments for routes and rest areas
  3. Driver Requirements
    • 30 min rest after every 4.5 hours driving
    • Max 10 hours driving/day, 56 hours/week
    • 360° walk-around before journey
    • No unauthorized passengers or route changes
  4. Journey Execution
    • Pre-trip briefing and JMP signing required
    • Strict tracking of driving/rest hours
    • VTS monitors every 3 hours
  5. Communication Protocol
    • Daily driver check-ins when parked
    • No phone use while driving - second driver handles communication
    • Emergency contacts must be notified immediately
  6. Journey Closure
    • Review of compliance with JMP
    • Counselling for violations
    • Update JMP based on driver feedback

OBC/VTS Procedure
  1. VTS Monitoring
    • Maintain vehicle tracking records daily
    • Verify vehicle locations with drivers
    • Record driver violations (speed, harsh braking, etc.)
    • Ensure proper use of Driver Identification Keys
    • Report FOB malfunctions to service providers
    • Prevent parking at blackspot areas
    • Maintain trip logs for all operations
  2. MDVR Monitoring
    • Ensure all vehicles have working MDVR devices
    • Perform spot checks as per plan
    • Monitor MDVR operations frequently
    • Record daily MDVR reports
    • Report malfunctions to service providers
    • Use video clips for driver training
    • Provide incident videos within 1 hour
  3. Geo-Fencing
    • Identify blackspots through RHM, NMPI reports, etc.
    • Collect latitude/longitude of blackspots
    • Set 100m geo-fence with tracker provider
    • Contact drivers stopping >5 minutes at blackspots
    • Record details for investigation
    • Interrogate drivers post-journey
  4. Violation Consequences
    • Mobile phone while driving: 7-day suspension (1st), 15-day block (3rd)
    • No seatbelt: 3-day suspension (1st), 7-day block (3rd)
    • Smoking while driving: Immediate blacklisting
    • MDVR tampering: ₹5000 fine (1st), ₹10000 (2nd), blacklist (3rd)
    • Product theft: Permanent blacklisting + ₹1M penalty
    • Unauthorized driver: Blacklisting at all locations

Operation Management Procedure
  1. Daily Operation Process
    1. Vehicle Availability
      • Confirm vehicle suitability with drivers/maintenance
      • Notify client via email
    2. Pre-Trip Inspection
      • Monitor driver inspects vehicle
      • Complete pre-trip checklist
    3. Loading Process
      • Supervisor collects dip-chart
      • Supervises loading completion
    4. Journey Preparation
      • Driver collects expenses
      • Journey desk provides JMP briefing
      • Pre-trip briefing against each invoice
    5. Journey Execution
      • VTS staff monitors via OBC/VTS/MDVR
      • Maintain journey communication logs
    6. Destination Arrival
      • Driver notifies Operation Manager
      • Supervisor stays in contact during unloading
    7. Return Process
      • Driver completes product unloading checklist
      • Presents at journey closing desk upon return

Staff Recruitment Procedure
Procedures – Summary
  • Recruitment based on merit; no discrimination by caste, creed, color, race, or language.
  • Mandatory criteria for applicants:
    • Age between 18 and 65 years
    • Physical requirements
    • Gender requirements
    • Experience requirements
    • Minimum education/training/certification
    • Two references
  • Recruitment process:
    1. Line Manager defines required criteria.
    2. OPS/HSEQ Manager forwards hiring request to Operations Director.
    3. Approval from Director/HSEQ/Operations Manager.
    4. Collection of applications and required documents.
    5. Written test and technical interview by HSE Manager.
    6. Final interview by Director; issue employment offer.
    7. On acceptance, sign offer letter and confirm Date of Joining.
  • Employee orientation on joining:
    • Submit educational documents and experience certificates.
    • Issue necessary items (laptop, mobile, SIM, etc.).
    • Conduct orientation program and introduce employee to organization.
