Emergency Procedures

Product Spillage

Driver Duties:

  • Park at safe location, inform ERP, client, fire brigade
  • Shut off engine, use spill kit or seek help
  • Cordon area, place cones, keep public away

ERP Coordinator:

  • Inform HSEQ, log incident, dispatch response team
  • Coordinate with police, ambulance, ATS

HSEQ & CEO: Support team, investigate, report to client, protect company reputation

Fire

Driver: Attempt to extinguish, inform ERP and fire brigade

ERP Coordinator: Dispatch support, arrange product transfer

HSEQ & CEO: Investigate, notify insurance, report to client

Medical Emergency

Driver: Provide first aid or call ambulance

ERP Coordinator: Arrange resources, log incident

HSEQ & CEO: Support team, investigate, report

Vehicle Collision / Rollover

Driver: Report injuries, shut off engine, control spill

ERP Coordinator: Notify authorities, arrange transfer, log incident

HSEQ & CEO: Investigate, inform stakeholders, review procedures

Vehicle Breakdown

Driver: Park safely, inform ERP, cordon off area

ERP Coordinator: Call maintenance or towing support

HSEQ & CEO: Monitor actions, inform management, update records

Brake Failure

Driver: Park safely, report issue, stay on site

ERP Coordinator: Arrange product transfer if required

HSEQ & CEO: Monitor and document actions, ensure follow-up

Hijacking / Terrorism

Driver: Do not resist, move to safe location, report

ERP Coordinator: Coordinate with police, tracking, calm driver

Law & Order Situation

Driver: Park safely, inform ERP, calm mob, seek police help

ERP Coordinator: Update HSEQ, coordinate with local law enforcement

HSEQ & CEO: Provide support, assess risk, ensure continuity
